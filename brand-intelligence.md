## Typography evidence and decision
Accenture publicly uses Graphik as a distinctive institutional typeface. No proprietary font files are copied or committed. The campaign uses a documented licensed/system substitute stack (`Segoe UI`, `Aptos`, `Helvetica Neue`, sans-serif) and reproduces the brand's bold modern-grotesk behavior through tight display tracking, strong weight contrast, lowercase wordmark preservation and spacious white fields. The substitute is not represented as official.

## Photography / illustration
No generic stock photography is used. Recognition comes from the official wordmark, purple/white/black palette, sharp forward geometry and high-contrast editorial composition. The bridge/load-path system is original candidate artwork derived from the role's production-deployment work.

## Motion character
The role-derived task pulse traverses the complete deployment bridge. Scenario choices alter contract strength and the path resolves to release, shadow/human-first or hold. Motion is purposeful, keyboard-accessible through native buttons and fully resolved under reduced-motion preferences.
